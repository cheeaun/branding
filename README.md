Writing
---

My full name is **Lim Chee Aun**. **Lim** is the surname/family name/last name. **Chee Aun** is the give name/first name. **cheeaun** is the nickname and always lowercased.

### Correct

- Lim Chee Aun
- Chee Aun
- cheeaun

### Incorrect

- Lim Chee
- Chee
- Lim Aun
- Cheeaun (uppercased 'C')
- limchee
- ca
- CA
